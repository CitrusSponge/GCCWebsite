Clash Score is a group of fans of Clash of Clans, playing since 2012. Their goal is to provide the competitive data of Clash of Clans in an organized and interactive way, providing the ability to analyze and make decisions based on data.

Our Sponsors

Global Clash Cup appreciates all the support it receives and is especially proud to announce our current season sponsors. Please show them support by subscribing and following their social media channels.

KreekandClash is a Clash Of Clans streamer. Always bringing fun content and new concepts, he decided to give Global Clash Cup a shot and sponsor the league.

Agam is a Clash Of Clans Base Builder for Vatang. His bases are highly recommended and sort-after by the community. If you need any, join his Discord server!

Champ Bases, a division of Clash Champs, offers affordable professional bases, tested against various on and off meta attacks, from Base Building Professionals to help players win at every level of the game.

THE BEERMAN is a content creator showcasing defense and base building with his weekly "beat up beer" stream, with prizes rewarding those who beat his bases!
