Our Sponsors

The Global Clash Cup appreciates all the support it receives and is especially proud to announce our current season sponsors. Please show they support by subscribing and following their social media channels.

KreekandClash is a Clash Of Clans streamer. Always bringing fun content and new concepts, he decided to give the Global Clash Cup a shot and sponsor the league.

Agam is a Clash Of Clans Base Builder for Vatang and Activit-E. He is a contracted builder for RH BaseBuilding and iBuild. His bases are highly recommended. If you need one, message him on. Discord.

The Team iBuild is an organization composed of very experienced base builders & reviewers from top clans (Blaze JP, Activit-E, Vatang and many more). They also propose coaching sessions for a very low price and soon a summer license with many benefits!
