What is Global Clash Cup?

Global Clash Cup (GCC) is a competitive league in Clash Of Clans with organized play opportunities across a range of Town Hall levels. Multiple divisions are offered each season to accommodate shifting interests within the Clash Of Clans community.

At the core of GCC is a strong commitment to Fair-Play with a minimum amount of administrative overhead to achieve a Fair-Play environment. We strive to make it as easy as possible for clans to participate in organized and fair wars, and are continuously innovating to reduce the administrative burdens on clans and on league staff.

GCC is a welcoming community, encouraging participation by new clans as well as those with extensive league play experience. GCC has a staff of about 25 volunteers. Rouletabille, is the founder, creator, and leader of the GCC. Residing in France, he has been a dedicated Clash Of Clans player for 7 years, and is committed to continuously improving the league operation experience for both the players and the staff.
